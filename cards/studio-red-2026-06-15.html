DB Deadlift
LADDER 1→6
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, a DB in each hand held in front of the thighs, knees soft. Back straight, reach your butt to the rear wall as deep as comfortable, then stand tall.
Coaching Cues
›"Hips back — the bells just ride the legs down"
›"Flat back, proud chest"
›"Push the floor away to stand"
›"Squeeze the glutes at the top — don't lean back"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Lower Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth — stop where the back stays flat.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds.

Explosive Bench Press Up
LADDER 1→6
▼
Movement
Stand at the side of the FitBench. Fall toward the bench, hands catching your body, and take your chest to the bench top. Explode as you press up and away from the bench back to standing. Repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Catch yourself with soft elbows — absorb, don't crash"
›"Chest all the way to the bench top"
›"Explode off — push the bench away from you"
›"Body stays in one line — no sagging hips"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierPress up without the explosive push-off — keep hands on the bench.
⬆️
HarderAdd a clap or press from a lower surface.
🩹
Wrist IssuesCatch on fists or reduce the fall distance.
💥
Box Jump
LADDER 1→6
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width apart. Squat back like you're sitting in a chair, then explode up to jump onto the bench. Step down and repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Load the hips, swing the arms — explode up"
›"Land soft in a quarter squat — quiet feet"
›"Full foot on the box every time"
›"Step down, never jump down"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Calves Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep up instead of jumping, or use a lower target.
⬆️
HarderAdd height or pause briefly between reps to reset power.
🩹
Knee IssuesReplace with step ups — no jumping.

🔔
KB Goblet Squat
LADDER 1→6
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, single KB held in front of the chest. Sit back, chest tall, as deep as possible. Stand squeezing the glutes, repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Elbows inside the knees — push them out"
›"Sit into it — chest tall the whole way down"
›"Drive through the heels, squeeze at the top"
›"Own the bottom — don't bounce out of it"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or go bodyweight.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range, toes slightly out.
🔁
TRX Single Arm Row
LADDER 1→6 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Hold one handle in one hand with the arm straight, feet hip-width. Pull the hand to the armpit while staying square to the anchor, then lower to the start.
Coaching Cues
›"Stay square — fight the twist the whole pull"
›"Pull the elbow straight back, not out wide"
›"Plank from heels to head"
›"Lower slow — don't unwind at the bottom"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Biceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk the feet back to stand more upright.
⬆️
HarderWalk the feet forward, or pause at the top of each row.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range and keep the elbow path comfortable.
🔔
KB Tactical Lunge
LADDER 1→6 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Holding the KB at your side, complete a reverse lunge with the opposite foot and pass the KB underneath the front leg at the bottom. Step forward and repeat on the opposite side.
Coaching Cues
›"Pass the bell under the front thigh at the bottom"
›"Stay tall — don't dive down to make the pass"
›"Back knee hovers, doesn't slam"
›"Smooth handoff — the bell never rests"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Core Obliques
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLunge without the pass, holding the bell at the chest.
⬆️
HarderSlow the descent and pause for the pass.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth and shorten the step.
