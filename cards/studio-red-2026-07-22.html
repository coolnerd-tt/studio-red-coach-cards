Fitband Anti- Rotation Press
LADDER 12→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Start standing sideways with the hands in the middle of the chest, holding the FitBand handles. Push the hand straight out, keeping the body from rotating, then bring the hands back to the chest and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Press out slow — the band wants to spin you"
›"Squeeze the glutes — lock the hips square"
›"The further out the hands, the harder it fights"
›"Breathe out as you press"

Core Obliques Shoulders Chest
Modifications

⬆️

KB Shoulder to Shoulder Press
LADDER 12→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Feet shoulder-width, KB held in front of the right shoulder with elbows pointing down. Press the KB up and over your head, then bend the elbows and slowly lower it to the left shoulder. Keep alternating shoulders.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Press up and over — draw a rainbow with the bell"
›"Slow lower to the other shoulder — that's the work"
›"Ribs down, glutes tight — no leaning side to side"
›"Catch soft — bell lands gently every time"
Muscles
Shoulders Triceps Core Obliques
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the bell or press straight up without the travel.
⬆️
HarderPause overhead at the top of each arc.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to eye level and shrink the arc.

KB Step Back Swing 1 arm
LADDER 12→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Start in a staggered stance, left foot back, KB in the left hand. Hinge at the hips to take the KB between the legs, then snap the hips forward, swinging the KB and stepping forward simultaneously. Step the leg back and let the KB fall back between the legs again, moving together. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Step and swing together — one motion"
›"Hips drive the swing, not the arm"
›"Flat back on every hinge"
›"Land the step as the bell floats"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Shoulders
Modifications
⬇️
EasierSwing in place without the step.
⬆️
HarderBigger step, heavier bell.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesLighten the bell and shorten the backswing.

Res. Band Split Stance Row Alt.
LADDER 12→8
▼
Movement
In a stance slightly longer than a split stance, set up with the resistance band under the front foot. Row each hand to the rib/hip area. Slowly return to the starting position. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues

›"Row to the ribs, elbow tight"
›"Control the return — don't let the band snap you"
›"Alternate hands smoothly"
