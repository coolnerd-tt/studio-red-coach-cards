🏋️ Bench
💪
DB Single Leg Deadlift
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Stand on the right leg holding the DB with both hands. Reach the left heel to the rear wall, keeping a straight line from ankle to ear. Squeeze the right glute to stand. Switch legs and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Reach the heel back, not down"
›"One line — ankle to ear"
›"Hips square to the floor the whole way"
›"Squeeze the standing glute to rise"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Core Lower Back
Modifications
⬇️
EasierKickstand stance — back toe lightly down.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower or pause at the bottom.
🩹
Balance IssuesHold a rack or wall with the free hand.
💪
DB Bent Over Row Knee on Bench
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Left knee and hand on the bench, back flat, right foot on the floor. DB in the right hand with a neutral grip, pull the DB from under the shoulder up toward the hip, then return. Switch sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Flat back — torso parallel to the bench"
›"Pull toward the hip pocket, not the armpit"
›"Elbow tight to the body"
›"No twisting — hips stay square"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Biceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the DB and shorten the pull.
⬆️
HarderPause 1-2 seconds at the top.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesKeep the supported setup and avoid rounding.
🎗️
Fitband Crossover Y Raise
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Standing at the opposite end of the Fitbands, cross them and hold a handle in each hand. Pull the arms up and out to create a 'Y' at the top, then control back to the start and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Cross the bands — pull up and out to the Y"
›"Straight arms, thumbs up"
›"Squeeze the blades down and back at the top"
›"Slow return — don't let the bands snap you down"
Muscles
Rear Delts Upper Back Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep toward the anchor to reduce tension.
⬆️
HarderStep back, pause at the top of the Y.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesRaise to shoulder height instead of a full Y.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB Offset Rack Cossack Squat
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Holding the KB in the front rack on one side, step wide and complete a Cossack squat — sitting into one hip with the other leg straight. Switch sides when it's time to switch.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Sit into one hip — the other leg stays long, toes up"
›"Bell racked tight — don't let it pull you over"
›"Chest tall as you sink"
›"Push through the whole foot to come back up"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Adductors Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBodyweight Cossack, holding a rack for balance.
⬆️
HarderDeeper range or pause at the bottom.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth — a half-range Cossack still counts.
🏐
Ball Push Ups
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Starting in a high plank with one hand on the ball, complete a push up. Drop the knees to the ground to modify intensity.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Stack the shoulder over the ball — own the wobble"
›"Chest drops level between the hands"
›"Squeeze the glutes — one straight line"
›"Push through the ball just as hard as the floor"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierDrop to the knees, or both hands on the floor with the ball put aside.
⬆️
HarderRoll the ball to the other hand between reps.
🩹
Wrist IssuesBoth hands on the ball, neutral wrists.
🏐
Ball Skater hops w/ Pause
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Standing on the left foot with the right leg lifted, hop to the side and land on the right leg, the left foot sweeping behind to balance. Let the ball follow the outside leg as you jump side to side. Pause after each hop. Repeat the opposite way.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Stick every landing — pause and own it"
›"Push off the outside edge, land soft"
›"Ball swings with you — let it lead the rhythm"
›"Stay low — hips back like a skater"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Calves Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep side to side instead of hopping, tap the toe behind.
⬆️
HarderBigger hops, longer pause on one leg.
🩹
Knee IssuesSmall lateral steps only — no jumping.
