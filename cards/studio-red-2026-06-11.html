DB Bird Dog Row
10 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
DB in the right hand, left hand and right knee on the bench, left leg extended. Pull the DB to the ribs, lower, and repeat — keeping a straight line from ankle to ear. Switch sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"One line, ankle to ear — don't let the hips sag"
›"Row to the ribs, elbow tight"
›"The extended leg stays squeezed and still"
›"Slow lower — balance is half the exercise"

Ball Clean to Press
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Feet shoulder-width with the med ball in the middle. Sit the hips back, grab the ball, then drive the feet into the floor and thrust the hips forward, bringing the ball to the chest. Press it to the ceiling, return it to the floor, and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips do the clean — the arms just guide it"
›"Stand all the way up before you press"
›"Press straight overhead — finish tall"
›"Flat back every time you reach for the floor"
Muscles
Glutes Shoulders Quads Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierClean to the chest only — skip the press.
⬆️
HarderHeavier ball or add a squat before the press.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to eye level instead of full overhead.

Ball Half Push up
10 REPS
▼
Movement
In a high plank with the med ball under the chest, complete a half push up, touching the chest to the ball, then press back to the top and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Chest taps the ball — same depth every rep"
›"Elbows about 45 degrees — not flared wide"
›"One straight line — squeeze the glutes"
›"Push the floor away hard at the top"
