DB Step Up alt.
LADDER 15→10 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Hold a DB in each hand. Step up onto the box with one leg, stand all the way tall, then step down with the same leg. Alternate legs each rep.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Drive through the WHOLE foot on the box — push the floor away"
›"Stand all the way tall at the top — don't cut the rep short"
›"Control the step down — lower, don't drop and crash"
›"Knee tracks over the toes — don't let it cave in"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Calves Core Grip
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DBs or bodyweight. Lower the box. Hold the rack for balance.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DBs. Higher box. Pause 1 sec balanced at the top before stepping down.
🩹
Knee IssuesLower the box to reduce knee bend. Bodyweight only. Drive through the heel and avoid any pain range.
🔔
KB Thruster
LADDER 15→10
▼
Movement
Hold the KB in the front rack position. Sit back into a squat, chest tall. Drive the heels down and thrust the hips forward to standing, driving the weight to the ceiling. Return to the rack and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbow stays HIGH in the rack — don't let it drop in the squat"
›"Legs power the press — one drive from the floor up"
›"Stand and press in the SAME beat — one fluid motion"
›"Lock out overhead — bicep by your ear, ribs down"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Shoulders Triceps Core Upper Back
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter KB, or hold one KB in both hands. Split into a squat then a press. Reduce squat depth.
⬆️
HarderHeavier KB. Pause 1 sec at the bottom of the squat before driving up.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to shoulder height only — no overhead. Lighter KB.
💪
DB Plank Row
LADDER 15→10 · ALTERNATE SIDES
▼
Movement
In a high plank with a hand on each DB, brace the core and pull one DB up to the ribs. Lower it back to the plank with control, then switch sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squeeze glutes and brace — hips stay LEVEL, no rotation"
›"Pull the DB to the RIBS — elbow drives back, not out"
›"Wide feet for a stable base — build a tripod"
›"Slow and controlled — don't fling the weight up"
Muscles
Upper Back Lats Core Biceps Shoulders Glutes
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DBs. Widen the feet, or drop to the knees. Row without the DB to groove the pattern.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DBs. Narrow the feet. Pause 1 sec at the top of each row.
🩹
Wrist IssuesUse hex DBs or push-up handles to keep the wrists neutral. Lighter load.
🤸
BW Push Up
LADDER 15→10
▼
Movement
Feet on the floor, back flat, hands under the shoulders. Lower the chest to the floor and press back up to the start. Elevate the hands on a bench if needed.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Body is one straight line — squeeze glutes and brace the core"
›"Elbows at 45° — don't flare them straight out to the sides"
›"Chest leads all the way down — earn the full range"
›"Press the floor away and lock out tall at the top"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Shoulders Core Glutes Serratus
Modifications
⬇️
EasierElevate the hands on a bench or rack. Or drop to the knees. Reduce the range.
⬆️
HarderElevate the feet. Slow 3-second lower. Pause 1 sec at the bottom.
🩹
Wrist IssuesUse push-up handles or fists to keep the wrists neutral. Elevate the hands.

Fitband Triceps Extensions
LADDER 15→10
▼
Movement
Hands start overhead. Bend at the elbow as the hands come down behind the head. Press the hands away, extending the arms back to start, while maintaining a strong core position.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbows point straight to the ceiling — pin them, don't flare"
›"Only the forearms move — upper arms stay locked"
›"Full lockout at the top — squeeze the triceps"
›"Brace the core — don't arch the back to gain leverage"
Muscles
Triceps Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep closer to the anchor to reduce band tension. Or grip the band shorter.
⬆️
HarderStep further from the anchor. Slow 3-second lower. Pause at the bottom stretch.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range — only lower halfway behind the head. Less band tension.

Ball Power Slam w/ Hop
LADDER 15→10
▼
Movement
Start with the med ball between the feet. Pick it up in a squat and hop up as you raise it overhead, then slam it down by sending the hips back, driving the ball between the big toes.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Explode UP on the hop — fully extend hips, knees and ankles"
›"Reach TALL overhead before the slam — load the whole body"
›"Hips go BACK on the slam — hinge, don't just bend the arms"
›"Catch the bounce and reset fast — keep the rhythm"
Muscles
Shoulders Core Glutes Lats Quads Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter ball. Skip the hop — stay grounded and slam. Smaller overhead reach.
⬆️
HarderHeavier ball. Higher hop. Faster cycle rate for conditioning.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter ball. Hinge from the hips with a flat back. Smaller range and no hop.
