🏋️ Bench
💪
DB SL Hip Thrust on Bench
12 REPS
▼
Movement
Upper back on the bench, legs bent. Drive one heel into the ground and squeeze the glutes to lift the hips as high as possible on one leg. Lower with control, then switch legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"One heel drives — the other leg is just a passenger"
›"Squeeze at the top — full hip lockout"
›"Hips stay level — don't let the free side drop"
›"Ribs down, chin tucked"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierTwo-leg hip thrust, or keep the free foot lightly down.
⬆️
HarderAdd a DB across the hips or pause at the top.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce the range — no arch at the top.
💪
DB Shoulder Press
12 REPS
▼
Movement
Hold the DBs at or in front of the shoulders. Legs locked, core braced. Press the DBs toward the ceiling, return to the start, and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Press straight up — finish with biceps by the ears"
›"Squeeze the glutes — no leaning back"
›"Ribs down — brace before every press"
›"Lower with control to the shoulders, not past them"
Muscles
Shoulders Triceps Upper Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the DBs or press seated with back support.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds, or press one arm at a time.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress in a neutral grip to a comfortable height.
🤸
Alt. Lying Leg Raise
12 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Lying flat, hands under the glutes if needed. Start with both legs straight up. Lower one leg at a time with control until just before it taps the ground, raise it back up, and alternate.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Low back glued down — that's the whole game"
›"One leg works, the other holds tall"
›"Lower slow — hover, don't touch"
›"Keep both legs long"
Muscles
Lower Abs Core Hip Flexors Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBend the moving knee to shorten the lever.
⬆️
HarderLower both legs together, or add a pause at the bottom.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShrink the range — keep the legs higher.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB T-rex Swing
12 REPS
▼
Movement
KB on the floor slightly in front of you, feet shoulder-width. Slide the hips back, grab the handle, and hike it between the legs. Drive the hips forward and stand tall, bending at the elbows to let the bell float to chest height, then drive it back between the legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips snap, elbows bend — bell floats to the chest"
›"It's still a hinge — don't squat the swing"
›"Keep the bell close on the pull"
›"Flat back on every hike"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Upper Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStandard two-hand swing without the elbow pull.
⬆️
HarderHeavier bell — keep the float at chest height.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesLighten the bell and shorten the backswing.
🏐
Ball Slam
12 REPS
▼
Movement
Start with Med Ball between feet. Pick up in a squat and slam by sending hips to back wall between big toes.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squat to pick up — back flat, chest tall"
›"Load the hips and slam with intent — full body explosion"
›"Slam between your big toes — not in front, not to the side"
›"Retrieve and reset — don't chase the bounce"
Muscles
Full Body Core Lats Shoulders Glutes Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter ball. Less aggressive slam — controlled drop instead.
⬆️
HarderHeavier ball. Add a jump at the top before the slam.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter ball. Reduce squat depth. Focus on flat back on pickup.
🔁
TRX Low Row
12 REPS
▼
Movement
Grab the handles with both hands and lean backwards. With your palms facing up towards ceiling, pull yourself up to the handles. The handles should end up right under your chest. Release yourself back out.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Palms up — supinated grip hits the biceps and lower lats"
›"Body stays in one line — no hip sag, no piked butt"
›"Pull elbows down and back — handles land under your ribs"
›"Slow release — earn the negative"
Muscles
Upper Back Lats Biceps Rear Delts Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk feet back — more upright = less load. Bend knees.
⬆️
HarderWalk feet forward — more horizontal = more load. Pause 2 sec at top.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range. Don't pull as deep — stop when elbows reach 90°.
