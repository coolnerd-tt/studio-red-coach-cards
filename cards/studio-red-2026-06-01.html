🏋️ Bench
💪
DB SL Deadlift Row Alt.
▼
Movement
Stand on one foot, DBs in hands. Hinge at the hips and push them back, back flat, soft bend in the standing knee. At the bottom, row the weights to your hips, then lower. Extend the arms, stand tall, and switch sides.
Coaching Cues
›"Hinge from the hips — push them back, don't squat down"
›"Square your hips to the floor — don't let the lifted side open up"
›"Row to the hip, elbow tight — squeeze the back at the top"
›"Stay balanced — own the standing leg before you switch"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Upper Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierTap the back toe down for balance instead of staying fully single-leg.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom of the hinge before rowing.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce the hinge depth and keep the back flat — never round to reach lower.
💪
DB Devils Press
▼
Movement
Holding DBs at your sides, set them on the floor under your shoulders and step or jump back to a plank. Lower your chest to the floor, then step or jump the feet back in. In one continuous movement, swing the DBs between your feet and snatch them overhead to lockout.
Coaching Cues
›"Chest all the way to the floor — full rep every time"
›"Use your hips — this is a swing into a snatch, not a curl"
›"Punch the dumbbells straight overhead and stand tall"
›"Find a steady rhythm — pace it so you don't gas out"
Muscles
Shoulders Glutes Chest Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep back and in instead of jumping, and press the DBs up instead of snatching.
⬆️
HarderMove continuously with heavier DBs for an unbroken set.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to a comfortable height instead of a full overhead lockout.
🤸
Leg Raises on bench
▼
Movement
Lie flat on the bench, hands under your glutes if needed. Start with both legs straight up. Lower them together with control until just before they tap the floor, then raise back to the top and repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Press your low back into the bench the whole time"
›"Control the lower — that's where the work is"
›"Keep the legs long — only a soft bend in the knees"
›"Stop the lower before your back arches off the bench"
Muscles
Lower Abs Core Hip Flexors Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBend the knees to 90° to shorten the lever.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds with the legs just off the floor each rep.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce the range — keep the legs higher and never let the back arch.
🔵 Rack
🤸
BW Popcorn Squat
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, drop into a squat and add a small jump, staying down in the lower range. Keep holding the low squat stance and 'pop' continuously without standing all the way up.
Coaching Cues
›"Stay low — live in the bottom of the squat"
›"Small, springy jumps — quick off the floor"
›"Chest tall, weight in your heels"
›"Soft landings — knees track over your toes"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Calves Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierSkip the jump — pulse in the bottom of the squat instead.
⬆️
HarderDrop lower or hold the position longer between pops.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range and remove the jump.
🔔
KB Lateral Lunge Alt.
▼
Movement
Stand feet hip-width with a KB at your chest. Step one leg out to the side and sit back into that hip and heel, keeping the other leg straight. Press through the foot to return to standing, alternating sides.
Coaching Cues
›"Sit back into the hip — push the glute behind you"
›"Working knee tracks over the toes, other leg stays long"
›"Chest tall — don't fold over the bell"
›"Drive through the whole foot to stand"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Adductors Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or hold the KB at the chest with no added load.
⬆️
HarderPause at the bottom or hold the bell lower in a goblet position.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth and keep the knee tracking behind the toes.
🔁
TRX Pike
▼
Movement
High plank with feet in the TRX straps, hands under shoulders. Keeping the legs straight, drive your hips up toward the ceiling as far as possible, then lower back to a strong plank with control.
Coaching Cues
›"Hips to the ceiling — pike up, don't just tuck"
›"Push the floor away — strong shoulders"
›"Legs long — squeeze the quads"
›"Control the lower — don't sag the hips at the bottom"
Muscles
Core Hip Flexors Shoulders Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierDo a TRX knee tuck instead of a straight-leg pike.
⬆️
HarderSlow the tempo — 3 seconds up, 3 seconds down.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesSubstitute a floor-based pike with feet on a slider.
