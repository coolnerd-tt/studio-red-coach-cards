🏋️ Bench
💪
DB Single Arm Deadlift
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, a DB in one hand held in front of the thighs, knees soft. Back straight, reach the butt to the rear wall as deep as comfortable, then drive through the heels to stand.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips back — the bell just rides down"
›"Flat back, proud chest"
›"Squeeze the glutes to stand"
›"Keep the bell close to the leg"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Lower Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or use both hands on one DB.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShorten the range and lighten the load.
💪
DB Incline Chest Fly Alt.
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Lie on the incline bench, feet on the floor, holding the DBs above the chest with a slight bend in the elbows. Drop one arm at a time, opening it as though hugging the ceiling, then return and repeat on the opposite side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"One arm flies while the other holds steady"
›"Soft elbows the whole way"
›"Open wide, but don't overstretch the shoulder"
›"Control the return — squeeze at the top"
Muscles
Chest Front Delts Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the DBs or shorten the range.
⬆️
HarderBoth arms together for a bigger stretch load.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce the fly depth to a comfortable range.
🎗️
Fitband Triceps Extension
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Hands start overhead. Bend at the elbow as the hands come down behind the head. Press the hands away, extending the arms back to start, while maintaining a strong core position. ⚠️ Use the same setup as the video.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbows point to the ceiling, stay tight to the head"
›"Only the forearms move"
›"Full extension at the top"
›"Brace the core — don't let the band pull you"
Muscles
Triceps Core Shoulders
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep toward the anchor to reduce tension.
⬆️
HarderStep back, pause at full extension.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range — stop before full overhead.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB Row in Gorilla Alt.
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Wide stance, hips back, back straight, one arm hanging straight with the KB in the other hand. Row the KB to the ribs, return slowly, and repeat on the opposite side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hold the hinge — the torso doesn't bob"
›"Row to the ribs, elbow past the back"
›"Shoulders square — don't twist to lift"
›"Slow return, quiet bell"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Core Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStagger one hand on a bench for support.
⬆️
HarderPause at the top of each row.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesRaise the bells onto a riser to reduce the hinge depth.
🏐
Ball Squat
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Holding the med ball in front of your body, sit the hips down until the thighs are parallel with the ground. Stand tall and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Sit the hips down and back — chest stays tall"
›"Thighs to parallel — full depth every rep"
›"Drive through the heels to stand"
›"Ball stays tight to the chest — don't let it drift"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or squat to a bench target.
⬆️
HarderAdd a pause at parallel or press the ball overhead as you stand.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range, toes slightly out.
🔁
TRX Crossing Balance
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Begin feet hip-width, holding the TRX handles at 90 degrees. Step backward, crossing the knee behind the other leg, knee almost to the ground, torso upright. Return, switch legs, and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Cross behind — not straight back"
›"Torso stays upright, don't lean into the straps"
›"Knee hovers, doesn't slam down"
›"Balance through the whole rep"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Core Adductors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierShorten the cross-step and use the straps for more support.
⬆️
HarderReduce hand support and slow the tempo.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range.
