DB RDL To Power Clean
▼
Movement
Stand feet shoulder width apart, DB in each hand in front of shins. Hinge at hips and complete RDL. Then at the top, drive feet into the floor and thrust hips forward, bringing DBs just above shoulders. Bring DBs back to floor, repeat combo.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hinge first — feel the hamstring stretch before you reverse"
›"Power comes from the hips — explode through the floor"
›"DBs stay close to your body — zip the jacket on the way up"
›"Catch soft on the rack — elbows up and around the DBs"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Shoulders Lower Back Traps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DBs. Or split into separate movements — RDL first, then a separate clean.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DBs. Add a press at the top of each clean.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLight DBs. Reduce range — only pull to mid-shin. Focus on the hinge pattern.
💪
DB Chest Press to Pullover
▼
Movement
A DB in each hand, palms facing the feet. Complete a chest press, then rotate the hands to face one another and, keeping a slight bend in the elbows, extend the DBs overhead until the elbows reach the temples. Return over the chest and repeat the combo.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Press first, then pull the ceiling back over your head"
›"Ribs down on the pullover — don't let the back arch"
›"Soft elbows, long reach"
›"Two moves, one rhythm"
Muscles
Chest Lats Triceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the bells — the pullover sets the weight.
⬆️
HarderSlow the pullover phase to 3 seconds.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesShorten the overhead reach to a comfortable range.
💪
DB Upright Row
▼
Movement
Starting with the DBs in front of the thighs, pull the elbows up and back to shoulder height, then return.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbows lead — they finish above the wrists"
›"Pull up the body line — bells stay close"
›"Stop at shoulder height — no higher"
›"Lower slow — same path down"
Muscles
Shoulders Upper Back Biceps Forearms
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the DBs and shorten the pull.
⬆️
HarderPause 1-2 seconds at the top.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPull only to chest height with a wider hand path.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB Side Lunge Alt.
▼
Movement
Stand feet hip-width with a KB at the chest. Step one leg out to the side and sit back into that hip and heel, keeping the other leg straight. Press through the foot to return to standing, alternating sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Sit back into the hip — push the glute behind you"
›"Working knee tracks over the toes, other leg stays long"
›"Chest tall — don't fold over the bell"
›"Drive through the whole foot to stand"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Adductors Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or hold the KB at the chest with no added load.
⬆️
HarderPause at the bottom or hold the bell lower in a goblet position.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth and keep the knee tracking behind the toes.
🔁
TRX Overhead Squat
▼
Movement
Start with arms fully extended overhead and in the straps holding tension. Squat down keeping the arms overhead and tension in the straps, then stand and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Arms stay locked overhead the whole squat"
›"Keep tension in the straps — don't let them go slack"
›"Chest tall, sit between the heels"
›"Full stand to reset"
Muscles
Quads Shoulders Core Glutes
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce squat depth or lower the arm height slightly.
⬆️
HarderPause at the bottom of the squat.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesHold the arms at a comfortable height instead of full overhead.
🔔
KB Alt. Halo to Woodchop
▼
Movement
Hold the KB by the horns. Extend it to the left hip pocket, raise it to the right ear and around the head (halo), then extend to the right hip pocket and halo from the left ear. Repeat the pattern.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Chop to the pocket, circle the head, chop to the other pocket"
›"Keep the bell close on the halo — tight circle"
›"Rotate from the ribs on the chop"
›"Slow and smooth — this is a control drill"
Muscles
Obliques Shoulders Core Upper Back
Modifications
⬇️
EasierSplit it — all chops, then all halos.
⬆️
HarderHeavier bell, slower circles.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesSkip the halo — chop pocket to pocket only.
