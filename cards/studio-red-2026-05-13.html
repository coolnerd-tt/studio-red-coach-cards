Bulgarian Offset Weight Split Squat
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
DB held in front rack position on one side with same leg up on bench, keep torso upright and complete a Bulgarian Split Squat. Stand and repeat. Complete on other side too.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Offset load — DB on the SAME side as the working leg makes it spicier"
›"Torso stays tall — no lean forward to make it easier"
›"Drop straight down — back knee tracks toward the floor"
›"Drive through the front heel to stand"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Core Adductors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBW only or lighter DB. Reduce depth. Hold rack with free hand for balance.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom. Heavier DB. Slower 3-second lower.
🩹
Knee IssuesReduce depth significantly. Keep front shin more vertical. BW only.

DB Donkey Kicks on Bench
8 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
In tabletop position, raise one leg directly off the bench with DB in crook of knee. Lower with control, complete reps, then switch sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Pinch the DB in the back of the knee — don't drop it"
›"Drive the heel to the ceiling — squeeze the glute hard at the top"
›"Hips stay square — no rotation when you kick up"
›"Slow on the lower — control the descent"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Lower Back
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBW only — no DB. Reduce range — kick up to hip height only.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DB. Add a pulse at the top. Slow 3-second lower.
🩹
Low Back IssuesBW only. Smaller range — don't arch the back to gain height.

Push Up
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Start in high plank position, body in a straight line. Slowly lower chest close to floor, push the floor away to come back up.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Body in one line — head to heels, no sagging hips, no piked butt"
›"Elbows at 45° — not flared out to the sides"
›"Chest to the floor — full range, full chest contact at the bottom"
›"Push the FLOOR away — full lockout at the top"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Shoulders Core Serratus
Modifications
⬇️
EasierHands on bench for incline push-up. Or drop to knees on the floor.
⬆️
HarderFeet elevated on bench. Slow 3-second lower. Add a clap at the top.
🩹
Wrist IssuesPush-up handles or on fists. Or do them on a bench at incline.

DB Tate Press
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Lie on bench with DB in each hand, arms fully extended. Bend at and push elbows out, lowering DBs in towards chest. Press back up to start and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues

›"DBs come down toward your sternum — palms face your feet"
›"Full lockout at the top — squeeze the triceps"
›"Control the lower — don't crash the DBs on your chest"

Triceps Chest Shoulders

KB Swing
8 REPS
▼
Movement
KB on floor slightly in front of you, feet shoulder width. Hips slide back, grab KB handle, hike back between legs. Drive hips forward and stand tall, let bell float, then drive back between legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips are the engine — NOT your arms"
›"Hike the KB high between your legs — get it past your hips"
›"Snap to standing — full hip extension, glutes squeezed, abs tight"
›"Let the bell float — don't muscle it up with your shoulders"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Shoulders Lower Back Grip
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter KB. Lower swing height — chest level only. Or sub a KB deadlift to learn the hinge.
⬆️
HarderHeavier KB. American swing (overhead). Add a 1-sec pause at the top.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter KB. Smaller range. Sub deadlifts if any back discomfort.

TRX Low Row
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Grab the handles with both hands and lean backwards. With your palms facing up towards ceiling, pull yourself up to the handles. The handles should end up right under your chest. Release yourself back out.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Palms up — supinated grip hits the biceps and lower lats"
›"Body stays in one line — no hip sag, no piked butt"
›"Pull elbows down and back — handles land under your ribs"
›"Slow release — earn the negative"
Muscles
Upper Back Lats Biceps Rear Delts Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk feet back — more upright = less load. Bend knees.
⬆️
HarderWalk feet forward — more horizontal = more load. Pause 2 sec at top.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range. Don't pull as deep — stop when elbows reach 90°.

Ball Slam
8 REPS
▼
Movement
Start with Med Ball between feet. Pick up in a squat and slam by sending hips to back wall between big toes.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues

›"Load the hips and slam with intent — full body explosion"
›"Slam between your big toes — not in front, not to the side"
›"Retrieve and reset — don't chase the bounce"
Muscles
Full Body Core Lats Shoulders Glutes Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️

⬆️
HarderHeavier ball. Add a jump at the top before the slam.
🩹
