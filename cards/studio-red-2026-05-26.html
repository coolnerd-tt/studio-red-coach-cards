DB Thruster 1arm Alt.
EMOM · ALTERNATE ARMS
▼
Movement
Hold DB in front rack position. Sit back into a squat, chest tall. Drive heels down and thrust hips forward to standing and drive the weight to the ceiling. Return to start.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbow stays HIGH in the rack — don't let it drop on the squat"
›"Use the legs — the press is powered from the hips, not the arm"
›"One fluid motion — stand and press in the same beat"
›"Lockout overhead — bicep by your ear, ribs down"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Shoulders Triceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DB. Or split into squat then press as two movements. Reduce squat depth.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DB. Pause 1 sec at the bottom of each squat before driving up.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to shoulder height only — no overhead. Lighter DB.

DB Bent Over Fly
EMOM
▼
Movement
Stand tall, then hinge back. Hold DBs with palms facing. Squeeze shoulder blades and raise DBs to shoulder level and lower. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hinge first — chest down, flat back, hold that position"
›"Lead with the elbows — not the hands"
›"Squeeze the shoulder blades together at the top"
›"Slow lower — control the negative"
Muscles
Rear Delts Upper Back Traps Core Lower Back
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DBs. Rest chest on a high bench for support and to take the back out.
⬆️
HarderSlow 3-second lower. Pause 1 sec at the top. Heavier DBs.
🩹
Low Back IssuesReduce hinge depth — keep torso more upright. Or do it seated on the end of the bench.
🎗️
Fitband Tricep Extension
EMOM
▼
Movement
Hands start overhead. Bend at the elbow as hands come down behind the head. Press hands away, extending arms back to start, while maintaining a strong core position.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Elbows point straight to the ceiling — pin them, don't flare"
›"Only the forearms move — upper arms stay locked"
›"Full lockout at the top — squeeze the triceps"
›"Brace the core — don't arch the back to gain leverage"
Muscles
Triceps Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep closer to the anchor to reduce band tension. Or grip the band shorter.
⬆️
HarderStep further from anchor. Slow 3-second lower. Pause at the bottom stretch.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range — only lower halfway behind the head. Less band tension.

TRX Lat Pull Seated
EMOM
▼
Movement
Adjust handles to highest position. Sit on the floor with your legs bent at the knee or straight. Bring your ribs to your hips and pull your body up toward the anchor point using the straps. Lower controlled. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Pull with the LATS — feel the shoulder blades drop and pull down"
›"Ribs to hips — engage the core to come up as one piece"
›"Drive elbows DOWN — not back like a row"
›"Slow lower — earn the negative on every rep"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Biceps Core Rear Delts
Modifications
⬇️
EasierKnees bent and feet flat to assist. Or scoot closer to anchor for less load.
⬆️
HarderLegs straight. Scoot further from anchor. Pause 2 sec at the top.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range. Smaller pull height — don't force through any pinch.

KB Swing w/ Lateral Step
EMOM · ALTERNATE SIDES
▼
Movement
KB Swing while taking a lateral step, alternating sides from right to left.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Step out as the KB swings UP — timing makes the move flow"
›"Land balanced before the next hinge — don't rush the step"
›"Hips power the swing — not the arms"
›"Stay athletic — soft knees, chest tall through the float"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Shoulders Adductors Grip
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter KB. Skip the step — stay planted, just swing.
⬆️
HarderHeavier KB. Larger lateral step for more travel. Quicker tempo.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter KB. Smaller range — chest-height swings only.

Squat to Split Jump
EMOM
▼
Movement
Complete a squat, then a split jump lunge on each side and back to start. Continue to repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squat first — full depth, full standup before the split jump"
›"Switch FULLY in the air — not on the ground"
›"Land soft into the lunge — back knee almost grazes the floor"
›"Reset to squat between split jumps — that's one rep"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Calves Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep into the lunges instead of jumping. Reduce squat depth.
⬆️
HarderAdd height to the squat jump. Hold DBs at sides. Faster tempo.
🩹
Knee IssuesStep switch instead of jumping. Reduce squat depth.
