🏋️ Bench
💪
DB SL Hip Thrust on Bench
10 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Upper back on the bench, legs bent. Drive one heel into the ground and squeeze the glutes to lift the hips as high as possible on one leg. Lower with control, then switch legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"One heel drives — the other leg is just a passenger"
›"Squeeze at the top — full hip lockout"
›"Hips stay level — don't let the free side drop"
›"Ribs down, chin tucked"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierTwo-leg hip thrust, or keep the free foot lightly down.
⬆️
HarderAdd a DB across the hips or pause at the top.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce the range — no arch at the top.
💪
DB Squeeze Press
10 REPS
▼
Movement
Lie flat on the bench, a DB in each hand, the bells touching directly over the chest. Keep them squeezed together as you lower over the chest, elbows grazing past the ribs, then press back up. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Crush the bells together — never let the squeeze go"
›"Elbows graze the ribs on the way down"
›"Press and squeeze at the same time"
›"Slow lower — feel the chest the whole rep"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Front Delts Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the bells — the squeeze is the work.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom while squeezing.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesShorten the lower — keep the elbows above the bench line.

🔵 Rack
🤸
Res. Band good Morning
10 REPS
▼
Movement
Stand on the band, feet hip-width, and loop it around the back of the neck. Bend at the hips, then pull the hips through to stand. Drive the hips back to the start and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips back first — chest follows"
›"Flat back — proud chest at the bottom"
›"Squeeze the glutes through to stand"
›"Keep the band snug on the neck the whole time"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Lower Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth or use a lighter band.
⬆️
HarderSlow the descent to 3 seconds.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShorten the hinge range.
🔁
TRX Low Row
10 REPS
▼
Movement
Grab the handles with both hands and lean backwards. With your palms facing up towards ceiling, pull yourself up to the handles. The handles should end up right under your chest. Release yourself back out.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Palms up — supinated grip hits the biceps and lower lats"
›"Body stays in one line — no hip sag, no piked butt"
›"Pull elbows down and back — handles land under your ribs"
›"Slow release — earn the negative"
Muscles
Upper Back Lats Biceps Rear Delts Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk feet back — more upright = less load. Bend knees.
⬆️
HarderWalk feet forward — more horizontal = more load. Pause 2 sec at top.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range. Don't pull as deep — stop when elbows reach 90°.
🤸
Res. Band Hammer Curl
10 REPS
▼
Movement
Stand tall on the resistance band, palms facing each other holding the band in each hand. Curl to the shoulder and back down to the hips. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Neutral grip the whole rep"
›"Elbows pinned to the ribs"
›"Slow the lower — fight the band"
›"Stand tall, no swinging"

Biceps Forearms Core
