🏋️ Bench
🤸
Band DB RDL
▼
Movement
With the Fitband looped above the knees, stand feet hip-width, a DB in each hand held in front of the thighs, knees soft. Back straight, reach the butt to the rear wall as deep as comfortable.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Press the band out the whole rep"
›"Hips back — the bells just ride down"
›"Flat back, proud chest"
›"Squeeze the glutes to stand"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Adductors Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierRemove the band or reduce depth.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShorten the range and lighten the load.
💪
DB Pullover Hips Off Bench
▼
Movement
Lie on the bench, holding a DB with both hands over the chest. Lower it overhead keeping the ribs down, then pull it back to the starting position. Repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Ribs down — don't let the back arch"
›"Long arms, soft elbows"
›"Feel the stretch through the lats"
›"Pull back with the lats, not just the arms"
Muscles
Lats Chest Triceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierShorten the overhead range.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce the overhead reach to a comfortable range.
💪
DB Russian Twist on Bench
▼
Movement
Seated on the bench, chest high, hold the DB at the chest. Rotate and tap the weight to the floor, bring it back through center, and tap the other side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Rotate from the ribs, chest stays high"
›"Tap and go — don't rest at the floor"
›"Control both directions equally"
›"Feet can lift for an added challenge"
Muscles
Obliques Core Hip Flexors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierKeep feet down and reduce the rotation range.
⬆️
HarderLift the feet off the floor.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShrink the range and go slow.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB Swing w/ Lateral Step
▼
Movement
KB Swing while taking a lateral step, alternating sides from right to left.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Step out as the KB swings UP — timing makes the move flow"
›"Land balanced before the next hinge — don't rush the step"
›"Hips power the swing — not the arms"
›"Stay athletic — soft knees, chest tall through the float"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Shoulders Adductors Grip
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter KB. Skip the step — stay planted, just swing.
⬆️
HarderHeavier KB. Larger lateral step for more travel. Quicker tempo.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter KB. Smaller range — chest-height swings only.
🔔
KB Wave Squat
▼
Movement
Holding the KB in the goblet position, complete a squat, finishing with a calf raise at the top of the movement.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squat, stand, then rise to the toes — one wave"
›"Full squat first — don't shortchange it for the calf raise"
›"Balance tall at the top of the raise"
›"Heels down soft before the next rep"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Calves Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierSkip the calf raise or go bodyweight.
⬆️
HarderPause at the top of the calf raise.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit squat depth to a pain-free range.
🏐
Ball Half Push up
▼
Movement
In a high plank with the med ball under the chest, complete a half push up, touching the chest to the ball, then press back to the top and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Chest taps the ball — same depth every rep"
›"Elbows about 45 degrees — not flared wide"
›"One straight line — squeeze the glutes"
›"Push the floor away hard at the top"
Muscles
Chest Triceps Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierDrop to the knees and keep the same chest-to-ball depth.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower, or take a full-range push up with hands on the ball.
🩹
Wrist IssuesTurn the hands out slightly or use fists.
