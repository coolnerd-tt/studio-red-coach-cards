DB Thruster 1 arm
LADDER 8→12
▼
Movement
Hold the DB in the front rack position. Sit back into a squat, chest tall. Drive the heels down and thrust the hips forward to standing, driving the weight to the ceiling. Return to start.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squat first, then punch it overhead"
›"Full squat before the drive"
›"Stand tall before the press finishes"
›"Catch soft on the way back down"

DB Step Through Clean
LADDER 8→12 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Stand feet shoulder-width, DB in the right hand in front of the shins. Drive the feet into the floor and thrust the hips forward, bringing the DB just above the shoulder while simultaneously tapping the opposite foot to the bench top. Return the DB and foot to the floor, then repeat on the second side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips snap the bell up — the arm just guides"
›"Tap the bench as the bell lands — one beat"
›"Catch soft at the shoulder"
›"Flat back every time you reach down"
Muscles
Glutes Shoulders Quads Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierClean without the foot tap.
⬆️
HarderHeavier bell or quicker cycle.
🩹
Balance IssuesSkip the tap and keep both feet planted.

Reverse Sit Up on Bench
LADDER 8→12
▼
Movement
Lie on the bench, grab the bench above the head. Legs as straight as possible, reach the feet to the ceiling, letting the hips come off the bench slightly, and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips lift, legs stay long"
›"Small range — control beats height"
›"Grip the bench for stability"
›"Exhale as the hips lift"
Muscles
Lower Abs Core Hip Flexors
Modifications

EasierBend the knees to shorten the lever.
⬆️
HarderSlow the tempo and pause at the top.
🩹

TRX Single Arm Row
LADDER 8→12 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Hold one handle in one hand with the arm straight, feet hip-width. Pull the hand to the armpit while staying square to the anchor, then lower to the start.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Stay square — fight the twist the whole pull"
›"Pull the elbow straight back, not out wide"
›"Plank from heels to head"
›"Lower slow — don't unwind at the bottom"

⬆️

TRX Torso Rotation Short lever
LADDER 8→12 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Start standing sideways with the hands in the middle of the chest. Rotate the upper body to face the anchor point, keeping the hands close to the chest and arms tucked into the body. Rotate back to the side and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Short lever — hands stay glued to the chest"
›"Rotate from the ribs, not just the arms"
›"Feet stay planted — only the torso turns"
›"Control both directions equally"
