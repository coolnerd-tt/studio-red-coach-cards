🏋️ Bench
💪
DB Split Squat
15 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Split stance, DBs at the sides or shoulders. Drop the back knee almost to the ground, torso tall, then drive through the front heel to stand. Switch feet at the halfway point.
Coaching Cues
›"Drop straight down — like an elevator, not an escalator"
›"Front knee tracks over the toes"
›"Chest tall — no leaning into the front leg"
›"Push the floor away through the front heel"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierUse bodyweight only or reduce depth.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom, or front-rack the DBs.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range.
🎗️
Fitband Hip Thrust
15 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Fitband connected low on the bench and across the hips, upper back on the bench, legs bent. Drive the heels into the ground and squeeze the glutes to lift the hips as high as possible. Lower with control.
Coaching Cues
›"Drive through the heels — toes can even lift"
›"Squeeze the glutes hard at the top — full lockout"
›"Ribs down — don't arch the low back to finish"
›"Chin tucked, eyes forward over your knees"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierRemove the band and thrust bodyweight only.
⬆️
HarderPause 2-3 seconds at the top of each rep.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce the range — lift to a height with no back arch.
💪
DB Push Press
15 REPS
▼
Movement
Hold the DBs in the front rack position. Dip and drive with the legs, using the momentum to help press the DBs toward the ceiling to lockout. Bend the knees as you bring the weights back to the rack.
Coaching Cues
›"Short dip, big drive — the legs start the press"
›"Punch straight up — finish with biceps by the ears"
›"Heels stay down on the dip"
›"Absorb the catch — soft knees on the way down"
Muscles
Shoulders Quads Triceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStrict press lighter DBs without the leg drive.
⬆️
HarderPause overhead, or slow the lower to 3 seconds.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesPress to a comfortable height instead of full lockout.
🤸
V-Ups on Bench
15 REPS
▼
Movement
Lie on your back, arms straight overhead, legs extended. Sit up and lift the legs, touching hands to shins, knees, or toes. Slowly lower back down.
Coaching Cues
›"Fold in half — hands and feet meet in the middle"
›"Reach tall, not just forward"
›"Slow lower — that's the half that builds the core"
›"Keep the legs as straight as you can manage"
Muscles
Core Lower Abs Hip Flexors Quads
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBend the knees — touch hands to shins in a tuck-up.
⬆️
HarderHold a light DB or slow the tempo.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesDo single-leg V-ups, keeping one foot down.
🔵 Rack
🔔
KB Sumo Squat
15 REPS
▼
Movement
Feet wide, toes pointed slightly out, KB held between the legs. Sink the hips down and back, then stand and repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Knees track out over the toes — push them wide"
›"Sit straight down between the heels"
›"Chest proud — the bell hangs, you don't lean"
›"Squeeze the glutes at the top"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Adductors Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierUse bodyweight or reduce depth.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the bottom or add a heavier bell.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth and keep the knees tracking over the toes.

TRX Elbow Body Saw
15 REPS
▼
Movement
Low plank on the elbows with feet in the stirrups. Keeping the body parallel to the floor, push your body 6–8 inches slowly forward and then back. Continue the motion.
Coaching Cues
›"Small saw — 6 to 8 inches, no more"
›"Brace before you move — ribs to hips"
›"Hips level — no sag, no pike"
›"Slow both directions — control is the rep"
Muscles
Core Shoulders Lower Abs Lats
Modifications
⬇️
EasierHold a static TRX plank without the saw.
⬆️
HarderLengthen the saw or slow the tempo further.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesDo the saw from a floor plank with feet down.
🤸
Alt. Arm KB Swing
15 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
KB on the floor in front of you, feet shoulder-width. Slide the hips back, grab the handle with one hand, and hike it between the legs. Drive the hips forward and stand tall — as the bell floats, switch hands, then drive it back between the legs.
Coaching Cues
›"Switch at the float — the bell is weightless up top"
›"It's a hinge, not a squat — hips back, then snap"
›"Free arm sweeps back like a skier"
›"Shoulders square — don't let the bell twist you"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core Forearms
Modifications
⬇️
EasierTwo-hand swings — skip the switch.
⬆️
HarderHeavier bell, or switch every other swing.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesLighten the bell and shorten the backswing.
🔁
TRX Single Arm row
15 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Hold one handle in one hand with the arm straight, feet hip-width. Pull the hand to the armpit while staying square to the anchor, then lower to the start.
Coaching Cues
›"Stay square — fight the twist the whole pull"
›"Pull the elbow straight back, not out wide"
›"Plank from heels to head"
›"Lower slow — don't unwind at the bottom"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Biceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk the feet back to stand more upright.
⬆️
HarderWalk the feet forward, or pause at the top of each row.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce range and keep the elbow path comfortable.
