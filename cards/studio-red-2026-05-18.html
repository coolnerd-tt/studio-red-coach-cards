DB 1arm Snatch w/ Foot on Bench
LADDER 8→4→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Stand feet shoulder width apart, DB in right hand in front of shins. Drive feet into the floor and thrust hips forward, bringing DB overhead while simultaneously bringing the opposite foot to tap the bench top. Bring DB and foot back to floor, repeat second side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Power from the hips — the snatch is a hip drive, not an arm pull"
›"DB and opposite foot move together — one coordinated rep"
›"DB stays close to the body on the way up — zip the jacket"
›"Lockout overhead — bicep by your ear, full extension"
Muscles
Shoulders Glutes Hamstrings Core Traps Hip Flexors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DB. Skip the bench tap — keep both feet down. Or break into a clean instead of full snatch.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DB. Pause overhead with the foot on the bench before resetting.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesFinish at shoulder height — no overhead. Lighter DB, controlled tempo.
💪
DB Bridge and Press on bench
LADDER 8→4→8
▼
Movement
Lie on back on bench, lift hips into bridge position, press DBs to sky and return under control.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hold the bridge HIGH the whole set — glutes stay switched on"
›"Press the DBs straight up over the chest — not toward the face"
›"Elbows down to ribs on the lower — full range"
›"Two muscle groups at once — don't let the hips drop to rest"
Muscles
Chest Glutes Triceps Shoulders Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter DBs. Or hold the bridge static and just press — lower hips between presses if needed.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DBs. Single-arm press (alternate). Slow 3-second lower.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce press range. Keep elbows closer to 45°. Lighter load.
💪
DB SL Hip Thrust
LADDER 8→4→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Upper back on bench or floor. Legs bent. Lift one leg. Drive the other heel into the ground and squeeze butt to lift hips as high as possible. Lower controlled. Repeat alternating legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Drive through the HEEL of the working leg — not the toe"
›"Squeeze the glute hard at the top — full hip extension"
›"Hips stay LEVEL — don't let the lifted-leg side drop"
›"Control the lower — 2 seconds down, no dropping"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBoth feet down (double-leg thrust). No DB — bodyweight only.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DB on the hip. Pause 2 seconds at the top of each rep.
🩹
Low Back IssuesBoth feet down. Smaller range — focus on the glute squeeze, not height.
🔔
KB Counterbalance Lateral Lunge
LADDER 8→4→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Start holding KB in goblet position. As you step to the side for a lateral lunge, push the KB out straight in front of the chest. Bring back to goblet and complete the combo on the other side.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"KB presses out as you lunge down — it counterbalances you"
›"Sit INTO the working hip — straight leg stays long"
›"Chest tall — the KB out front helps you stay upright"
›"Drive through the working heel to return to goblet"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Adductors Core Shoulders Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter KB. Keep KB in goblet — skip the press-out. Reduce lunge depth.
⬆️
HarderHeavier KB. Pause at the bottom with the KB pressed out.
🩹
Knee IssuesReduce depth. Keep the working shin more vertical. Lighter KB.

TRX Low Fly
LADDER 8→4→8
▼
Movement
Arms extended, feet under anchors. Keeping arms straight, pull handles apart toward hips. Slowly lower body back to start position.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Arms stay straight — this is a lat and rear delt move"
›"Body in one line — plank the whole time"
›"Pull to your hips, not just out to your sides"
›"Slow return — don't just fall back to start"

Lats Rear Delts Core Triceps

Ball Chest Slam
LADDER 8→4→8
▼
Movement
Hinge hips back, chest pass the ball into the floor, retrieve, repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hinge the hips back to load — then explode the chest pass"
›"Full power through the chest — drive the ball into the floor"
›"Brace the core on every slam — don't round the back"
›"Retrieve and reset — controlled pickup before the next rep"
Muscles
Chest Shoulders Core Triceps Lats
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighter ball. Smaller slam — controlled push instead of full power.
⬆️
HarderHeavier ball. More explosive — add a small jump on the retrieve.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesReduce force. Controlled push rather than a hard slam. Lighter ball.
