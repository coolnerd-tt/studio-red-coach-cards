DB Box Squat
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Holding a DB in the front rack position, stand feet hip-width at the end of the bench. Control the body down into a squat to sit on the bench, then stand up and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Control the sit — don't just drop onto the bench"
›"Chest tall the whole way down"
›"Tap and go — light touch, not a rest"
›"Drive through the heels to stand"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierUse a higher surface or go bodyweight.
⬆️
HarderPause on the bench for 2 seconds before standing.

Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range.

Popcorn Squat
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, drop into a squat and add a small jump, staying down in the lower range. Keep holding the low squat stance and 'pop' continuously without standing all the way up.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Stay low — live in the bottom of the squat"
›"Small, springy jumps — quick off the floor"
›"Chest tall, weight in your heels"
›"Soft landings — knees track over your toes"
Muscles
Quads Glutes Calves Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierSkip the jump — pulse in the bottom of the squat instead.
⬆️
HarderDrop lower or hold the position longer between pops.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range and remove the jump.
💪
DB Deadlift
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Feet hip-width, a DB in each hand held in front of the thighs, knees soft. Back straight, reach your butt to the rear wall as deep as comfortable, then stand tall.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hips back — the bells just ride the legs down"
›"Flat back, proud chest"
›"Push the floor away to stand"
›"Squeeze the glutes at the top — don't lean back"
Muscles
Hamstrings Glutes Lower Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce depth — stop where the back stays flat.
⬆️
HarderSlow the lower to 3 seconds.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesShorten the range and lighten the load.
💪
DB SL Hip Thrust
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Upper back on bench or floor. Legs bent. Lift one leg. Drive the other heel into the ground and squeeze butt to lift hips as high as possible. Lower controlled. Repeat alternating legs.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Drive through the HEEL of the working leg — not the toe"
›"Squeeze the glute hard at the top — full hip extension"
›"Hips stay LEVEL — don't let the lifted-leg side drop"
›"Control the lower — 2 seconds down, no dropping"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierBoth feet down (double-leg thrust). No DB — bodyweight only.
⬆️
HarderHeavier DB on the hip. Pause 2 seconds at the top of each rep.
🩹
Low Back IssuesBoth feet down. Smaller range — focus on the glute squeeze, not height.

TRX Atomic Push Up
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Starting in a high plank with feet in the stirrups, complete a push up. At the top, tuck the knees toward the chest, then extend back out. Repeat the combo.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Push up first, then tuck — two distinct moves"
›"Hips stay level through both phases"
›"Full extension before the next tuck"
›"Strong plank the entire combo"
Muscles
Chest Core Shoulders Hip Flexors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierPush up from the knees, tuck from the floor.
⬆️
HarderSlow the tuck-and-extend tempo.
🩹
Wrist IssuesPush up on fists or a slight incline.

Ball Slam
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Start with Med Ball between feet. Pick up in a squat and slam by sending hips to back wall between big toes.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Squat to pick up — back flat, chest tall"
›"Load the hips and slam with intent — full body explosion"
›"Slam between your big toes — not in front, not to the side"
›"Retrieve and reset — don't chase the bounce"
Muscles
Full Body Core Lats Shoulders Glutes Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️

⬆️
HarderHeavier ball. Add a jump at the top before the slam.
🩹
Low Back IssuesLighter ball. Reduce squat depth. Focus on flat back on pickup.

KB Row In Lunge
6 EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Step back into a lunge position with a soft bend in the front leg and the back leg straight, leaning slightly forward with the free hand on the front thigh. Extend the KB toward the floor, then pull it toward the chest and back down. Alternate and complete on both sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Hold the lunge — the lower body is frozen"
›"Row to the ribs, elbow tight"
›"Flat back — chest just above the front thigh"
›"Long arm at the bottom of every rep"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Glutes Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierRest the working hand on the thigh between rows.
⬆️
HarderPause at the top of each row.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesStand taller in the lean and lighten the bell.

TRX Bicep Clutch
6 REPS
▼
Movement
Face the anchor point, palms holding the handles facing each other, arms fully extended. Pull yourself toward your hands, bringing palms toward the armpits, elbows out but not above shoulder height. Clutch and hold, then release and repeat.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Pull the body to the hands, not the hands to the body"
›"Elbows stay below the shoulders"
›"Squeeze and hold at the top"
›"Control the release — don't just fall back"
Muscles
Biceps Upper Back Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk the feet back to reduce the angle.
⬆️
HarderWalk the feet forward or extend the hold.
🩹
Elbow IssuesShorten the range and skip the hold.
