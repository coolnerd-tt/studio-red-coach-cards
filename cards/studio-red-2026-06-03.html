DB Neutral Grip Row on Bench
LADDER 12→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Place one knee and hand on the bench, back flat, other foot on the floor. DB in the free hand with a neutral grip. Pull the DB from directly under your shoulder up toward your armpit, then lower with control. Complete reps, then switch sides.
Coaching Cues
›"Flat back — body parallel to the floor"
›"Pull to the hip pocket, elbow tight to your side"
›"Squeeze the shoulder blade at the top"
›"Don't twist — keep both hips square to the bench"
Muscles
Lats Upper Back Biceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the DB and reduce range to keep the back flat.
⬆️
HarderPause 1–2 seconds at the top of each row.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesKeep the supported position and avoid rounding to reach lower.
💪
DB 1arm Crossbody Reverse Lunge
LADDER 12→8 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
DB in one hand. Step back into a reverse lunge and reach the DB across your body toward the front foot's ankle. As you stand, return the DB to your side. Complete reps on one side, then switch.
Coaching Cues
›"Reach across the body — feel the stretch through the hip"
›"Front knee over the ankle, back knee drops straight down"
›"Drive through the front heel to stand"
›"Stay tall through the spine as you reach"
Muscles
Glutes Quads Core Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReach toward the knee instead of the ankle, or use no weight.
⬆️
HarderSlow the descent or increase the DB load.
🩹
Knee IssuesShorten the lunge and limit depth to a pain-free range.

Superman on Bench
LADDER 12→8
▼
Movement
Lie face-down on the bench with arms reaching overhead and legs extended. Contract through the back and glutes to raise the arms and legs a few inches, hold at the top 3–5 seconds, then lower with control and repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Lengthen first — reach long through fingers and toes"
›"Lift from the back and glutes, not by cranking the neck"
›"Hold the top — squeeze for the full count"
›"Keep the gaze down to keep the neck neutral"
Muscles
Lower Back Glutes Upper Back Hamstrings
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLift arms and legs separately instead of together.
⬆️
HarderExtend the hold or add a light DB in each hand.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesReduce lift height — keep it small and pain-free.

Ball Overhead Slam to Squat Jump
LADDER 12→8
▼
Movement
Lift the med ball overhead and slam it to the floor. Before picking it up to slam again, perform a squat jump. Repeat the slam-then-jump sequence.
Coaching Cues
›"Full overhead reach — load the slam from the hips"
›"Slam through the ball, hinge as it hits the floor"
›"Explode straight up on the squat jump"
›"Soft landing — absorb through the hips and knees"
Muscles
Shoulders Quads Core Glutes
Modifications
⬇️
EasierRemove the jump — stand up out of the squat instead.
⬆️
HarderUse a heavier ball and move continuously.
🩹
Knee IssuesReplace the squat jump with a controlled bodyweight squat.
🏐
Ball Adductor Squeeze Squat
LADDER 12→8
▼
Movement
Place the ball between your knees, toes pointing straight ahead. Squeezing the ball the whole time, sit down into a squat and stand back up, keeping constant pressure on the ball.
Coaching Cues
›"Squeeze the ball the entire rep — don't let off at the top"
›"Chest tall, weight through the heels"
›"Knees press in on the ball, not out"
›"Control down, drive up"
Muscles
Quads Adductors Glutes Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce squat depth while keeping the squeeze.
⬆️
HarderAdd a pause at the bottom or hold a DB at the chest.
🩹
Knee IssuesLimit depth to a pain-free range.
🔁
TRX Hinge to Overhead
LADDER 12→8
▼
Movement
Facing the anchor, hold a TRX handle in each hand with tension on the straps. Hinge at the hips with a flat back, then drive the hips forward to stand tall, finishing with the arms reaching overhead. Lower back to the hinge with control.
Coaching Cues
›"Hinge from the hips — push them back, soft knees"
›"Flat back the whole way down"
›"Snap the hips through and reach tall overhead"
›"Let the straps support you — stay in control, don't yank"
Muscles
Glutes Hamstrings Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierReduce range — hinge to a comfortable depth and reach to shoulder height.
⬆️
HarderSlow the tempo and pause at full overhead reach.
🩹
Lower Back IssuesKeep the hinge shallow and the back flat throughout.
