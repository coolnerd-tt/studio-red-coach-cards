🏋️ Bench
🤸
Plank with Knee to Elbow
▼
Movement
Holding a plank — elevate the hands if needed — bring the same-side knee to the elbow, return to plank, and repeat on the other side.
Coaching Cues
›"Knee to elbow — exhale as it comes in"
›"Hips stay level — no piking to reach"
›"Push the floor away the whole time"
›"Slow and deliberate beats fast and sloppy"
Muscles
Core Obliques Hip Flexors Shoulders
Modifications
⬇️
EasierElevate the hands on the bench.
⬆️
HarderPause 2 seconds at the elbow.
🩹
Wrist IssuesDrop to a forearm plank.
💪
DB Chest Press to Pullover
▼
Movement
A DB in each hand, palms facing the feet. Complete a chest press, then rotate the hands to face one another and, keeping a slight bend in the elbows, extend the DBs overhead until the elbows reach the temples. Return over the chest and repeat the combo.
Coaching Cues
›"Press first, then pull the ceiling back over your head"
›"Ribs down on the pullover — don't let the back arch"
›"Soft elbows, long reach"
›"Two moves, one rhythm"
Muscles
Chest Lats Triceps Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierLighten the bells — the pullover sets the weight.
⬆️
HarderSlow the pullover phase to 3 seconds.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesShorten the overhead reach to a comfortable range.
🎗️
Fitband Row to External Rotation
▼
Movement
Facing the FitBench holding the Fitbands, pull the elbows up to shoulder height, rotate the hands up until level with the face, return to shoulder height, and back to the start. ⚠️ Set up bands as shown in video.
Coaching Cues
›"Row high, then rotate — two beats per rep"
›"Elbows stay at shoulder height through the rotation"
›"Knuckles to the ceiling at the top"
›"Reverse it slow — rotation, then row, back down"
Muscles
Rear Delts Upper Back Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep toward the anchor and shrink the rotation.
⬆️
HarderPause at the top of the rotation.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesKeep the row only — skip the rotation.
🔵 Rack
🔁
TRX I-Y-T
▼
Movement
Start with arms extended and feet under the anchor. Keeping the arms straight, pull to an I position and return, pull to a Y position and return, pull to a T position. Repeat the sequence.
Coaching Cues
›"Straight arms — these are raises, not rows"
›"I overhead, Y diagonal, T out wide"
›"Squeeze the blades on every letter"
›"Body stays a plank — no hip hinge to cheat"
Muscles
Rear Delts Upper Back Shoulders Core
Modifications
⬇️
EasierWalk the feet back to reduce the angle.
⬆️
HarderWalk the feet forward — small angle changes are big here.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesKeep the T only and skip the overhead I.
🤸
Low Plank Squeeze on Ball
▼
Movement
With the hands on the ball, lower into a low plank. Squeeze the ball with the hands and keep tension through the shoulder blades.
Coaching Cues
›"Crush the ball — squeeze the whole hold"
›"One straight line — glutes tight"
›"Push up out of the shoulders — no sagging chest"
›"Breathe — don't trade air for tension"
Muscles
Core Chest Shoulders Lower Abs
Modifications
⬇️
EasierDrop the knees, keep the squeeze.
⬆️
HarderExtend the hold or lift one foot at a time.
🩹
Shoulder IssuesStandard forearm plank on the floor.
🤸
Sprinter Burpee
▼
Movement
Complete a burpee. When you hop up at the top, hit a sprinter stance — arms and legs in opposite drive position — then drop back down and repeat.
Coaching Cues
›"Finish every burpee in the sprinter pose — knee up, arms split"
›"Chest to the floor at the bottom"
›"Stick the stance — balance for a beat"
›"Alternate the lead knee each rep"
Muscles
Quads Core Chest Hip Flexors
Modifications
⬇️
EasierStep back and in, skip the push up.
⬆️
HarderAdd a tuck jump before the sprinter stance.
🩹
Knee IssuesStep through every phase — no jumping.
