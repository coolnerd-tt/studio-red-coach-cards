Movement

DB Goblet Bulgarian Split Squat
LADDER 10→15 · EACH SIDE
▼
Movement
Holding a DB in the goblet position, place one foot on the bench top behind you. Torso tall, drop into a lunge keeping the back knee under the hip, then drive through the front heel to stand.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Front foot far enough out — knee tracks over the ankle"
›"Drop straight down — back knee under your hip"
›"Chest tall — don't fold over the bell"
›"Front heel does the work — push the floor away"

Ball Woodchop
LADDER 10→15
▼
Movement
Hold the ball over one shoulder with arms straight. Lower it across the body to the opposite hip, as though swinging an axe to chop wood. Return overhead and repeat, then switch sides.
▶ Watch Video
Coaching Cues
›"Chop on a diagonal — shoulder to opposite hip"
›"Rotate from the ribs and hips, arms stay long"
›"Control the swing both directions — don't let it whip you"
›"Pivot the back foot as you chop through"
